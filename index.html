FORD v FERRARI

Sinopse

"Ford v Ferrari" tells the real-life story of Carroll Shelby, an American car designer, and Ken Miles, a British race car driver, who are tasked by the Ford Motor Company to build a revolutionary race car. The goal is to beat the Ferrari team, which had a dominant track record, in the 1966 24 Hours of Le Mans endurance race. They face challenges from corporate interference, the laws of physics, and their own personal struggles to achieve this ambitious goal.

Elenco

  • Christian Bale
  • Matt Damon
  • Remo Girone

Christian Bale plays Ken Miles, a brilliant yet headstrong British race car driver known for his unmatched skills and fierce passion for racing.

Matt Damon portrays Carroll Shelby, a legendary American automotive designer and former racing driver recruited by Ford to lead their Le Mans campaign.

Remo Girone plays Enzo Ferrari, the iconic founder of Scuderia Ferrari and a towering figure in motorsport history.
